Justin

AKA: AjaxMasta. Growing up with money isn't always easy. Coming from a family who made their billions buying and selling corporate real estate globally, Justin was used to certain lifestyle: fast cars, fast women and evenings on one of his parent's many yachts sipping champagne with the current hottest celebrity. In his spare time, he developed a passion for poking around in databases (that may, or may not have been his).

It was one of these very evenings, when he stumbled into a database belonging to tech-savvy, black-market import/export master Jose "El Diablo" Conrado. Unable to resist, he used his mastery of AJAX to shuffle "just a few things" to a personal address in the Maldives. Needless to say, he was caught. He now spends his time hiding out in Seattle as a student at Code Fellows until things blow over.
